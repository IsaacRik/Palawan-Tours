About Palawan Tours

Palawan Tours guarantees an unforgettable experience in San Vicente by guiding visitors through its untouched beauty, from the expansive Long Beach to the tranquil island hopping excursions. Our expert local guides provide in-depth insights into the area's natural wonders, including hidden lagoons, lush forests, and vibrant marine life. With personalized itineraries, guests can enjoy exclusive activities such as kayaking through crystal-clear waters and visiting remote villages to learn about local culture. Palawan Tours ensures that every moment in San Vicente is unforgettable, offering a seamless and immersive adventure in one of Palawan's most pristine regions.

San Vicente

A tropical paradise

San Vicente, located on the northwest coast of Palawan, is a hidden gem known for its vibrant local community and breathtaking natural beauty. The region boasts pristine beaches, lush forests, and crystal-clear waters, making it a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. Long Beach, the longest white sand beach in the Philippines, stretches over 14 kilometers, offering a serene escape.
